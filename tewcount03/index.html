Welcome to Total Extreme Wrestling's new resource site!
Homepage

TEW Count is a resource site for the popular wrestling simulator game Total Extreme Wrestling. We provide information on the game, its community and also a selection of the best moderations available for it.

We currently have 2 current time data packs available for download; the default Cornellverse database and also the T-Zone database. We also have 2 scenarios available for download.

We are also working on our own fictional database, entitled TEW Count. It is in a very early production stage and there is no news on when or even whether it will be released.

Aside from the game databases, we have a selection of miscellaneous independent picture packs put together by us. This has been done to ensure high quality pictures for the more popular workers and promotions.

This site also includes information on the RaveX team who have been working on their stat updates since Extreme Warfare Revenge.

All content here has been used with permission. We have chosen to include only the best moderations that are available at the moment.
