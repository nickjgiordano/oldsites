Habbo Safety

This information is a summary of Habbo Hotel UK's Safety pages.

The Habbo Way

To obide by the Habbo Way you must NOT do any of the following:

1. Use any hacking, scripting or editing programs whilst in the hotel (e.g. WPE and ArtMoney).
2. Steal another Habbos' credits or furni or try to scam them (this of course includes scam sites).
3. Use any bad language in the hotel. Sexually graphic and racist words are forbidden. You may a recieve a ban for use of unallowed words.
4. Pester or harass Habbos who do not wish to talk to you.
5. Ask for Habbos' personal details. This includes email addresses, passwords or any other information.
6. Type your own password out loud anywhere in the hotel (except of course when you are signing in or changing your details).
7. Give away, trade or sell your Habbo account.
8. Try to persuade another Habbo to break the Habbo Way.

Habbo Hotel's Privacy Pledge

Habbo Hotel assures users that their personal details are safe with their site and within the hotel. Any company that contacts you via access to these details will be doing so to inform you of special offers that could benefit you.
