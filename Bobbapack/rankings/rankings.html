Fansite Rankings
These are the BobbaPack Fansite Rankings! These ratings and reviews are based solely on our opinions of the sites. Each site has been ranked based on four factors.

Site & Layout: How it looks
The first thing each site is rated on is plainly how it looks. If it is both easy on the eye, but still eyecatching, then it's likely to score high.

Site & Layout: Technicality
The technical side of the layout; this means how the site works. This includes advanced things such as user systems, to simply how well the site is maintained to ensure things such as that all hyperlinks work correctly. Also, whether the site fits well in all browsers is considered. Also, if the site is full of different types of multimedia devices such as flash videos then that is likely to boost the rating.

Content: General content and originality
If the content is well-written, original and hopefully humourous if intended, then the site will of course score high in this category. This also includes the quality of radio DJs on the site and the general feel given from the regular group of posters on the forums.

Content: Depth
This section rates content amount. If there is a lot of content and features on the site then it will gain points from this category alone. This also includes things such as forums and radios.

NOTE: each fansite is rated on the whole. This means that if for example a site is intended to be solely news-based, it will naturally lose points, since it does not include things such as radio features.

The rankings up so far are just there from when the site was being designed. Proper reviews and rankings will be up soon!
