About Us

The Bobba Package is a new fansite that not only aims to provide Habbo users with all the latest news, safety tips and rare values, but also to promote a refreshing approach and point of view to the fansite community.

The safety section of the site includes information about how to follow the rules and stay safe on Habbo and how to protect your computer from any kind of virus, trojan or spyware.

Our Habbo content consists of rare values, guides, tips & tricks, goodies and screenshots. We hope to provide the most up-to-date Habbo content possible.

Our radio is complete with our full DJ list, stats, a shoutout/request system and the timetable!

The staff section of the site lists who works or helps out on the site. It also includes how you could become a part of the team.

Aside from the site, check out our forums! We are hoping to build a forum community with loyal members. Forum competitions will be held regularly.

The Bobba Package was designed by RES0 of Habbo Hotel UK. It was designed in Paint and coded in Notepad. For help with setting up a site and learning HTML, visit www.w3schools.com, a tutorial site.
