Threats

Viruses

Programs that delete and harm files. Can spread to other computers.

Trojans

Programs that access and in some cases take control of your computer.

Keyloggers

Programs that record what is typed at your computer. Allows other people access to personal information.

Adware / Spyware

Programs that monitor your computer. Can view personal information and bring up pop-ups.
