HugotonHosting Getting Started Guide
1. First thing to do is take care of your domain name and it's nameservers!
Nameservers are the redirectional servers that tell every connecting computer where your website is located. In this case the nameservers will tell the connecting client's internet browser to connect to HugotonHosting

If registering a new domain with HugotonHosting
Skip to step 2 as all nameserver activity is automated.

If you already own your domain, or are purchasing it elsewhere:
Contact your registrar through which you purchased the domain, request a switch in nameservers to:

ns1.hugotonhosting.com - 209.97.214.80
ns2.hugotonhosting.com - 209.97.214.81
REMEMBER: Once nameservers have been switched, it takes 24-72 hours for the internet to update!
2. Begin uploading your site!
Connecting via FTP:
Point your FTP program to ftp.yourdomain.com or your IP provided above if the
nameservers have not yet switched. Login with the supplied username and
password and begin uploading to the www folder. All scripts should be
placed in the cgi-bin folder.

Connecting via Online FileManager:
Login to your control panel with the url provided in your welcome e-mail, or by logging into the members area from the top of any HugotonHosting website.

Click on File Manager icon
Click on the www folder
Click upload folder
